How we work

Initial Consultation.

We acknowledge that each renovation or remodel project is distinct and needs a personalized strategy to ensure its success. We'll arrange a first consultation to talk about your project, budget, and timeline.

Proposal

After our consultation, we will send you a detailed proposal that includes the project scope of work and pricing.

Planning

After receiving your approval on the proposal, we will work with you in the design phase to plan the renovation or remodel project and establish a timeline.

Project Build-out

Once we have an agreed-upon plan, our team of experienced professionals will bring your vision to life. Our aim is to provide you with a seamless and stress-free experience that reflects your personal style and needs from beginning to end.
